/ Our Story

We started with a belief:
everyone deserves to age with dignity.

Founded

Columbus, Ohio

Born from personal experience. Driven by purpose.

Kindness Home Care Services LLC was founded on the understanding that finding good, reliable home care for a loved one is one of the most stressful experiences a family can face. We've lived it. And we started this agency to make it better, not just for our clients, but for the caregivers who do the work.

We operate in Columbus, Ohio and the surrounding area, serving seniors and adults who need support at home but want to remain in the place they know best. Our model is simple: connect the right caregiver with the right client, build a plan that truly fits, and show up every time.

We are a licensed, insured, and bonded home care agency. Every caregiver we place is thoroughly screened, background-checked, and covered under our general liability and workers' compensation policies. Families never bear the administrative or legal burden of employing a caregiver themselves.

We're privately owned, community-rooted, and entirely focused on the families we serve, not on investor returns or corporate metrics.

Our promise to every client

  • We will always arrive on time and communicate clearly
  • We will treat your home, your family, and your privacy with respect
  • We will adapt the care plan as needs change, no bureaucracy
  • We will keep family members informed and involved
  • We will never rush, never dismiss, and never make anyone feel like a burden
Mission & Values

What guides us every day.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, personalized, and reliable home care that enables individuals to live safely, comfortably, and with dignity in their own homes, while giving their families peace of mind.

Our Vision

A community where every aging adult has access to quality in-home care, and where caregiving is recognized as the dignified, skilled profession it truly is.

Kindness & Respect

We believe the quality of care is measured not only in tasks completed, but in the softness of a voice, the patience in a moment, and the genuine warmth brought to each interaction.

Personalized First

There is no standard client. Every person we serve has their own history, preferences, routines, and needs. We build care plans that reflect the individual, not a template.

Insurance & Compliance

We are fully insured, bonded, and compliant, so you can focus on what matters.

Protecting our clients, their families, and our caregivers is not optional, it is foundational. Below is a summary of the insurance coverage and compliance standards we maintain.

General Liability Insurance

Our general liability policy protects clients and their property in the event of accidental damage or injury occurring during the course of care. Coverage is maintained continuously and verified annually.

Coverage includes:

  • • Bodily injury to third parties
  • • Property damage in client's home
  • • Personal and advertising injury

Workers' Compensation Insurance

All caregivers are covered by our workers' compensation policy. If a caregiver is injured while working in your home, you have no financial or legal liability, we handle it fully.

What this means for you:

  • • No employer liability exposure
  • • No out-of-pocket medical costs for caregiver injury
  • • Full compliance with Ohio state requirements

Bonded

Our surety bond provides financial protection in the rare event of theft or dishonest acts by a caregiver while in a client's home. We maintain active bonding coverage at all times.

Coverage includes:

  • • Employee dishonesty protection
  • • Coverage while in client's residence
  • • Annual renewal and verification

Background Checks

Every caregiver undergoes a comprehensive background check before their first placement. We conduct criminal history, sex offender registry, and professional reference verifications.

Screening includes:

  • • Federal and state criminal history
  • • Ohio sex offender registry check
  • • Professional reference verification

Privacy & HIPAA Awareness

We take client privacy seriously. All caregivers are trained in maintaining client confidentiality. Health information shared with us is used solely for care planning and coordination purposes.

Our commitments:

  • • Caregiver confidentiality training
  • • Secure handling of health information
  • • No sale of client data, ever

Ohio Licensed Agency

Kindness Home Care Services LLC operates in compliance with Ohio state regulations governing home care agencies. Our license status is maintained and renewed as required by law.

Policies

Clear policies. No surprises.

We believe families deserve to know exactly how we operate. Our policies are designed to protect everyone, clients, families, and caregivers alike.

Client Privacy Policy

All personal and health-related information shared with Kindness Home Care is treated as strictly confidential. We collect only what is necessary to provide care, and we never sell, rent, or share client data with third parties.

Read Privacy Policy

Service Agreement

Before care begins, every client signs a service agreement that clearly outlines the scope of services, billing terms, caregiver assignment, cancellation policy, and the rights and responsibilities of all parties.

Request a sample agreement

Caregiver Code of Conduct

All caregivers agree to a strict code of conduct: professional behavior at all times, full confidentiality, no gifts or financial transactions with clients, mandatory incident reporting, and compliance with the client's care plan.

Incident Reporting Policy

Any accidents, injuries, changes in client condition, or unusual events must be reported immediately by the caregiver. Families are notified promptly, and we maintain a written incident log for every case.

Cancellation Policy

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a partial fee as outlined in the service agreement. Emergency situations are handled on a case-by-case basis with full understanding.
