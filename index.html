Non-Medical Home Care

No needles. No clinical setting.
Just compassionate help with daily life.

Non-medical home care means our caregivers assist with the everyday tasks that keep life comfortable and dignified, such as bathing, meals, companionship, and errands, without any medical procedures or prescriptions.

Perfect for families just starting their search. Our caregivers are warm, trained, and background-checked, available for short shifts, daytime, evenings, and weekends.

Our approach

Three steps to bring care home.

Get started
1

Submit a Care Request

Tell us about your loved one through our simple online form, including their needs, daily routine, and what matters most to them.

  • Takes less than 5 minutes
  • No commitment required
  • Available 24/7 online
2

Personal Consultation

We contact you within one business day to talk through your situation, answer questions, and begin matching your loved one with the right caregiver.

  • Response within 1 business day
  • Brief home safety assessment
  • Caregiver matching begins
3

Care Begins

Your matched caregiver arrives on time, background-checked, and ready to build a routine your loved one looks forward to every day.

  • Caregiver intro visit first
  • Personalized care plan in hand
  • Regular coordinator check-ins
