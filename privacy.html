Overview

Kindness Home Care Services LLC ("we," "our," or "the Company") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all individuals who interact with our services, including clients, their family members, job applicants, and visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information, and describes your rights with respect to that information.

By using our website or engaging our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us using the information at the end of this document.

This policy applies to information submitted through our contact form, care request form, job application form, newsletter sign-up forms, email, phone calls, and any follow-up communication related to those requests.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information depending on how you interact with us:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information such as name, phone number, email address, and mailing address
  • Care request details such as the client's name, living situation, requested services, preferred start date, care preferences, and any health-related details you choose to include
  • Employment application information such as work history, certifications, references, background-check consent, and resume uploads
  • Newsletter subscription details, usually your email address only
  • Payment and billing information when you contact us about private-pay services
  • Communications you send us by website form, email, phone, or follow-up messages

Information Collected Automatically

  • Limited technical request information processed by the third-party services used to load fonts, icons, and form infrastructure
  • Basic browser and device information that may be logged by those providers for security and delivery purposes

We do not use advertising trackers, social media pixels, or analytics dashboards on this website at this time.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or similar data protection laws apply, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): When you tick the consent checkbox on our forms, subscribe to our newsletter, or accept cookies, you give us explicit consent to process your data for the stated purpose. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us — withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.
  • Contractual necessity (Article 6(1)(b)): When you request care services or enter into a service agreement with us, we process your data as necessary to perform that contract.
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): We may process data where required to comply with applicable law, such as Ohio state regulations governing home care agencies.
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): We may process data for our legitimate business interests, such as preventing fraud and maintaining the security of our systems, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights.

The data controller for this website is: Kindness Home Care Services LLC, 133 Cameo Woods Drive, Delaware, Ohio 43015, USA.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, coordinate, and improve our home care services
  • To communicate with clients and their families about care plans and updates
  • To process care requests and respond to inquiries
  • To evaluate and process job applications
  • To process payments and send invoices
  • To send our newsletter if you have subscribed (you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • To protect the safety and security of clients, caregivers, and the public

We will not use your information for purposes materially different from those described above without first notifying you and, where required by law, obtaining your consent.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Caregivers assigned to your case: We share relevant care-related information with the caregiver(s) assigned to a client to enable them to provide appropriate care.
  • Healthcare providers: With your consent, we may share relevant health information with physicians or other providers involved in the client's care.
  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party vendors for functions such as Google Workspace tools, email delivery, resume storage, background check processing, and payment processing. These vendors may process data on our behalf and may not use it for their own independent marketing purposes.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of the Company, clients, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, client information may be transferred, subject to the same privacy protections described here.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google's CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests these fonts, Google may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome (Cloudflare CDN): We load icons from cdnjs.cloudflare.com. Cloudflare may log request metadata. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Apps Script, Google Sheets, and Google Drive (form submissions): Form data you submit through our contact, care request, job application, and newsletter forms is transmitted through a Google Apps Script endpoint and stored in Google Sheets. Resume uploads submitted through the job application form may also be stored in Google Drive. These services are provided through Google Workspace and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We use these services to receive and organize submissions, contact you about your request or application, and keep intake records for business operations. Access is limited to authorized members of Kindness Home Care Services LLC and trusted service providers who need that access to perform their role.

Health Information and Confidentiality

Health-related information shared with Kindness Home Care Services LLC is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. We recognize that health information is sensitive and we handle it accordingly.

While we are a private-pay home care agency and not a covered entity under HIPAA in all circumstances, we apply HIPAA-aligned best practices to the handling of any health-related information we receive. Caregivers are trained in client confidentiality and are prohibited from sharing client health information with unauthorized parties.

Health information is used solely for care planning, matching, scheduling, and coordination purposes and is shared only as described in the Sharing section above. We encourage visitors not to submit more medical detail through the website forms than is reasonably necessary for an initial intake conversation.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Password-protected Google Workspace tools used for intake and record handling
  • Role-based internal access to care requests, contact inquiries, and job applications
  • Secure handling of physical documents containing personal information
  • Regular review of our operational and security practices

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to use commercially reasonable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy and to comply with legal, regulatory, and business obligations.

  • Contact inquiries and newsletter requests: typically retained for customer service follow-up, relationship management, and recordkeeping unless you request deletion and we are not required to keep the record.
  • Care request and service-related records: retained as needed for intake, care coordination, business records, and any applicable legal or regulatory obligations.
  • Job application records: retained for recruiting, hiring decisions, compliance, and recordkeeping for a reasonable period unless a longer retention period is required by law.
  • Resume uploads: retained only as long as needed for recruiting and employment review purposes unless required for compliance or dispute-resolution reasons.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your information, subject to our legal and operational obligations.
  • Opt-out of marketing: You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly.
  • Portability: Where applicable, you may request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before the withdrawal.
  • Lodge a complaint: If you believe your data rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority in your country of residence.

To exercise any of these rights, including requesting deletion of information you submitted through our website forms, please contact us at kindnesshomecareservices@gmail.com or by phone at (614) 270-5166. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

For more detail on the third-party services that may set cookies, see the Third-Party Services section above.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
