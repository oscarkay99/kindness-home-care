24-Hour Live-In Care

For clients who require continuous oversight, our live-in caregivers become a steady, reassuring presence in the home, day and night. They sleep on-site and are available around the clock, so families can rest knowing their loved one is never alone.

  • Continuous overnight supervision and support
  • Assistance with morning and evening routines
  • Emergency response and family communication
  • Medication reminders and fall prevention
  • Companionship throughout the day

Home Health Aide (HHA)

Our Home Health Aides provide hands-on, compassionate assistance with personal care and daily activities, helping clients maintain comfort, safety, and as much independence as possible in the place they know best: home.

What is an HHA? A Home Health Aide (HHA) is a trained caregiver who supports clients with personal care, basic daily tasks, mobility, and comfort at home, providing a steady, reliable presence for clients and peace of mind for families.

  • Personal hygiene and bathing assistance
  • Mobility support, transfers, and fall prevention
  • Medication reminders and basic health monitoring
  • Light meal preparation and household tasks
  • Companionship and emotional support

State Tested Nursing Assistant Care

Our State Tested Nursing Assistants (STNAs) bring clinical training and genuine compassion to clients who need more advanced personal and health-related care, all in the comfort and familiarity of home.

STNA in Ohio: The State Tested Nursing Assistant credential is Ohio's equivalent of the CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant) in other states. All our STNAs hold current Ohio STNA certification.

  • Advanced personal care and hygiene assistance
  • Vital sign monitoring and health observations
  • Safe transfers, positioning, and mobility support
  • Wound care observation and skin integrity monitoring
  • Care plan coordination with healthcare providers

Medication Reminders

Staying on a consistent medication schedule is critical for many clients. Our caregivers provide timely, friendly reminders to ensure medications are taken as prescribed, keeping clients on track without any clinical intervention.

Important note: Our caregivers provide reminders only, not medication administration or preparation. For clients needing medication management, our STNA caregivers can provide closer health monitoring.

  • Timely prompts to take prescribed medications
  • Observing for any missed doses or concerns
  • Communicating with family about adherence
  • Pharmacy pickup assistance

Respite Care for Families

Family caregivers give so much of themselves, and they deserve time to rest, recharge, and attend to their own lives. Our respite care service steps in to provide scheduled breaks, knowing their loved one is in warm, capable hands.

  • Scheduled breaks during the day or evening
  • Overnight respite support when needed
  • Weekend respite arrangements
  • Full care continuity, same routines, same warmth
