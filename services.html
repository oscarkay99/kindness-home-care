24-Hour Live-In Care

For clients who require continuous oversight, our live-in caregivers become a steady, reassuring presence in the home — day and night. They sleep on-site and are available around the clock, so families can rest knowing their loved one is never alone.

  • Continuous overnight supervision and support
  • Assistance with morning and evening routines
  • Emergency response and family communication
  • Medication reminders and fall prevention
  • Companionship throughout the day

Companionship

Loneliness is one of the greatest challenges facing older adults. Our companionship service pairs clients with warm, engaged caregivers who genuinely listen, share stories, play games, and help bring color back to everyday life.

  • Genuine, unhurried conversation and listening
  • Shared meals and leisurely walks
  • Card games, reading, hobbies, and activities
  • Accompaniment to appointments and outings
  • Regular progress updates for the family

Errands & Daily Help

The small things add up. Our caregivers handle the practical tasks of daily life so clients can conserve energy for what brings them joy — while their home stays organized, clean, and running smoothly.

  • Grocery shopping and errand runs
  • Prescription pickups and pharmacy visits

Cooking & Grooming

Nutrition and personal hygiene are deeply connected to health and self-worth. Our caregivers prepare wholesome, home-cooked meals suited to each client's preferences and dietary needs — and assist with grooming routines that maintain dignity and comfort.

  • Home-cooked breakfast, lunch, and dinner
  • Diet-specific meal planning (diabetic, low-sodium, etc.)
  • Gentle bathing and personal hygiene assistance
  • Dressing, hair care, and oral hygiene
  • Skin care and incontinence support

State Tested Nursing Assistant Care

Our State Tested Nursing Assistants (STNAs) bring clinical training and genuine compassion to clients who need more advanced personal and health-related care — all in the comfort and familiarity of home.

STNA in Ohio: The State Tested Nursing Assistant credential is Ohio's equivalent of the CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant) in other states. All our STNAs hold current Ohio STNA certification.

  • Advanced personal care and hygiene assistance
  • Vital sign monitoring and health observations
  • Safe transfers, positioning, and mobility support
  • Wound care observation and skin integrity monitoring
  • Care plan coordination with healthcare providers

Daily Living Assistance

The activities of daily living — getting up, moving around, taking medications, staying active — can become challenging over time. Our caregivers provide gentle, respectful support that helps clients maintain their independence while staying safe.

  • Mobility assistance and fall prevention
  • Medication reminders (not administration)
  • Gentle range-of-motion exercises
  • Structured daily routines and schedules
  • Cognitive engagement and memory activities
